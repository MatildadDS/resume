About

After more than 10 years of professional experience in different industries, which today make sense and allow me to cover a wide range of complementary skills, I decided to make a radical career change and move to the side of digital doers.

It's above all a two-year experience in a tech startup allowing me to work alongside developers that gave me access to this new world filled with opportunities, incredible people and where there are always new things to learn.

So with the idea of being able to produce my own first web pages, prototypes and app, I started to learn coding and decided to join Simplon.co in November 2020.
This motivating environment always in ebullition confirms me in the idea that I found my way.

Currently in a training program to become a fullstack Javascript developer but with a strong appetite for front-end. I am now looking to deepen my knowledge through one year of apprenticeship in an agency like yours, starting in September 2021.

I am a fast learner with full energy, self-motivated and performance-driven type! And I believe my diverse professional experiences, and my multicultural background can also bring extra precious values.
